❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Questions About
CollegeCBT

How does the AI generate relevant exam questions for my specific course?+
CollegeCBT uses Anthropic's Claude AI, which is trained on a vast body of academic literature, textbooks, and past examination materials. When you select your institution type, course, level, and topic, the system constructs a detailed academic prompt that specifies your exact context. Claude then generates questions that are accurate, contextual, and aligned with the NUC, NBTE, or NCCE curriculum for that subject. Every question is freshly generated — you will never get the same question twice.
Is CollegeCBT suitable for all Nigerian higher institutions?+
Yes. CollegeCBT covers the entire Nigerian higher education landscape: all 309+ accredited universities (federal, state, and private) regulated by NUC; all 78+ polytechnics (federal, state, private) regulated by NBTE; all 163+ Colleges of Education regulated by NCCE; and Innovation Enterprise Institutions (IEIs) offering NID programs. Whether you are studying at 100 Level in a federal university, HND at a polytechnic, or NCE at a college of education, CollegeCBT has your course content.
What is the difference between the Exam Lab and Mock Exam mode?+
The Exam Lab is an open-ended practice environment where you generate one question at a time, with full explanations shown immediately after each answer. It's ideal for learning and deep understanding. The Mock Exam simulates real examination conditions — questions are timed (90 seconds each by default), auto-submitted on timeout, and no explanations are shown until after the entire exam. At the end, you receive a WAEC grade (A1–F9), a full performance analysis, and a question-by-question review. Use Exam Lab to learn; use Mock Exam to test your readiness.
How accurate and reliable are the AI-generated questions?+
CollegeCBT uses Claude Sonnet by Anthropic — one of the world's most capable AI models for academic tasks. The questions are generated with explicit academic context (course name, institution type, level, topic) and are reviewed through our Quality Evaluator layer before rendering. While no AI system is perfect, our users report very high accuracy rates. We also continuously refine our prompt engineering based on user feedback. For any question you believe is inaccurate, you can flag it directly from the exam interface.
How does payment work? Can I pay in Naira?+
Yes — all pricing is in Nigerian Naira (₦). We use Paystack, Nigeria's leading payment gateway, which supports debit cards (Verve, Mastercard, Visa), bank transfer, USSD, and POS payments. There is no need for a dollar card or foreign currency. The Free plan requires no payment at all. The Student Pro plan is ₦5,000 per semester or ₦8,500 per year. The Departmental plan is ₦25,000 per year for up to 200 students.
What is the AI Readiness Score and how is it calculated?+
The AI Readiness Score (0–100) is a composite metric calculated from your practice history. It weights your: (1) average correct-answer rate across all sessions, (2) consistency of performance (streak), (3) performance on timed mock exams vs. untimed practice, and (4) coverage of different topics within your course. A score of 75+ indicates exam readiness. The score updates after every session and is used to generate your grade prediction and personalized study recommendations.
What is "Results as a Service" (RaaS)?+
RaaS means we measure our success by your exam score — not by whether you use our software. Traditional EdTech sells subscriptions regardless of outcomes. CollegeCBT is built around outcome accountability: our AI tracks your grade trajectory, predicts your readiness, generates an Outcome Projection report, and continues providing targeted practice until you hit 75%. Pro students who complete 30+ mock exams and do not achieve 75% are eligible for a free semester extension — because your result is our result.
